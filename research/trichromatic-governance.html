What the current test proves

The repo artifact for March 30 is small but useful. It proves the hidden-band state is not collapsing into one visible-only signature.

  • Forging the visible band is not enough. The report marks forgery resistance as all_detected.
  • Attack energy is consistently higher. The measured attack average energy is 1.611 versus 1.2476 for benign samples.
  • The state space is larger than the visible band implies. The model records 504 combinatorial bits across tongues, bands, and bridge relationships.

Why this is in research, not on the storefront

Trichromatic governance is still weak as a primary detector in the hybrid harness. Its standalone recall is only 12 out of 200 attacks in the current overlap run. That is not storefront material. What is useful now is its role as a structural veto and anomaly signal layered underneath the gate.
