## 1. Federal Programs

### 1.1 SBIR / STTR (Small Business Innovation Research / Small Business Technology Transfer)

The SBIR/STTR programs are the single largest non-dilutive funding source for small tech companies in the US. Phase I awards are $50K-$275K; Phase II awards are $500K-$1.75M. Every federal agency with an extramural R&D budget >$100M runs one.

|-------|---------|

| **Deadline** | Multiple solicitation windows per year (typically Feb, Jun, Oct) |
| **Eligibility** | US small business (<500 employees), majority US-owned |
| **SCBE Alignment** | The 14-layer security pipeline, post-quantum cryptography, and adversarial cost scaling directly address DoD AI security requirements. DARPA CLARA overlap strengthens positioning. |
