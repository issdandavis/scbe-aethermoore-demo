Bounded theory lane

History + Bible theory, turned into a governed training packet.

This packet takes a narrow slice of the local theory corpus and turns it into reusable training data without pretending the whole body of notes is already finished mathematics or finished theology. Source lanes

Three local notes drove this packet.

Sacred Eggs Systems Model provided the governed-release framing: genesis control, pre-crypto admission, fail-to-noise behavior, and transmission binding.

Sacred Tongues - Emotional Architecture provided lineage, witness, covenant, memory, and emotionally legible roles for the tongues.

Everweave Timeline provided the history spine: sanctuary-building, archive logic, and the difference between deep history and present action.

Claim boundary

What this packet is not.

This is not a proof packet for the math notes. It is not a religious authority model. It is not a claim that all symbolic language in the vault belongs in production weights.

It is a narrow training slice for models that should reason better about governed release, memory, witness, lineage, sanctuary, and historical framing inside SCBE language.

What got extracted

Dataset shape

Genesis + covenant semanticsThe model learns to talk about authority that must be allowed to come into existence before runtime execution becomes meaningful.
Witness + memory semanticsThe packet reinforces Runethic-style witness language: carrying what came before, preserving lineage, and treating history as a real system constraint rather than background flavor.
Sanctuary + invitation semanticsThe packet keeps a recurring pattern from the notes: safety is built by shaping habitable space and invitation conditions, not only by issuing prohibitions.
Timeline disciplineThe history lane is compressed into a usable spine so the model can distinguish core chronology from lore-dump noise.
