Complex Phase (L1 -> L2)

L1 (Complex Embedding): Each word activates one or more tongue dimensions based on its semantic domain. "password" lights up Umbroth (Security). "calculate" lights up Cassisivadan (Compute). The phase diagram shows the complex-valued embedding before realification.

L2 (Realification): The complex vector is projected to real coordinates — the 6 tongue activations you see in the bar chart. This is the input to the rest of the pipeline.
