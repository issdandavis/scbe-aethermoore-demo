The Harmonic Wall

Adversarial cost scales as Rd2: exponential in the square of drift distance. Drag to rotate, scroll to zoom, and watch the wall become visually obvious.

H(d, R) = Rd2
d = drift distance from safe origin  •  R = base cost multiplier
Linear: d * R
Quadratic: d² * R
SCBE Harmonic: R^(d²)
