## Tips for Outreach

1. **Subject line:** Specific, curiosity-provoking, no clickbait
2. **First sentence:** Get to the point - what you do, why it's different
3. **Middle:** Why THEY should care (their pain point)
4. **Ask:** Specific, low-commitment (20-min call, not "buy our product")
5. **Follow-up:** Day 5, add value (video, article, insight)
6. **Don't:** Attach files, use "just checking in", be vague

**Response rate benchmark:** 5-10% for cold email. 20%+ for warm intros.
