What to send when something breaks

  • What you were trying to do
  • What you expected to happen
  • What actually happened
  • The exact command, page URL, or product name
  • The exact error text if there is one

Use this with any AI assistant

Copy this into the AI you are using:

I am using SCBE-AETHERMOORE. Help me troubleshoot one issue at a time. First ask me for the exact error text, the page or command I used, my OS, and what I expected to happen.

What to expect from support

Delivery failures and broken access routes should be treated first, because they block the product itself. Setup and AI-behavior issues come next, and they move faster when you include the exact command, page URL, or error text instead of a summary from memory.

Next support feature

Polly can become a sidebar support bot trained on the repo, manuals, pages, and delivery routes. The right shape is a repo-aware guide that helps users navigate what exists, not a bot that hallucinates missing pages.
